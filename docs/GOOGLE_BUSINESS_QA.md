### 1. How far is Villa Olimpia from the beach?
**Answer:** Villa Olimpia is just 500 meters from Spiaggia dei Gigli, reachable in about 7 minutes on foot. The beach is Blue Flag certified and part of the Capo Rizzuto Marine Protected Area.

### 2. Is the pool private or shared?
**Answer:** The pool is shared among all guests of the 9 apartments. It's a large pool (12x6 meters) with a relaxation area, solarium, and gazebo. Access is exclusive to Villa Olimpia guests.

### 3. Is parking included?
**Answer:** Yes, each apartment has a reserved free parking space within the property. The parking is private and secure, accessible 24/7.

### 4. Do the apartments have WiFi?
**Answer:** Yes, all apartments have free high-speed fiber WiFi (up to 150 Mbps). The connection is stable and covers outdoor areas (pool, garden).

### 5. How many guests can each apartment accommodate?
**Answer:** Villa Olimpia offers 9 apartments with varying capacity: studio apartments for 2-3 guests, two-bedroom apartments for 4 guests, and three-bedroom apartments for up to 6 guests. In total, the property can accommodate up to 40 guests.

### 6. How does check-in and check-out work?
**Answer:** Check-in is from 3:00 PM and check-out by 10:00 AM. We're flexible for special needs - contact us in advance for special requests. Check-in can be self-service with detailed instructions.

### 7. Do the apartments have air conditioning?
**Answer:** Yes, all apartments are equipped with independent air conditioning in every room. Additionally, all have private terraces or verandas to enjoy the sea breeze.

### 8. Can I bring pets?
**Answer:** Yes, Villa Olimpia is pet-friendly! We accept small and medium-sized pets. We just ask you to let us know at the time of booking. A supplement may apply.

### 9. How far is Le Castella?
**Answer:** Le Castella, with the famous Aragonese Castle, is only 3 km from Villa Olimpia (about 8 minutes by car). It's easily reachable by bicycle or with a pleasant walk along the coast.

### 10. Is there a supermarket nearby?
**Answer:** Yes, there are several supermarkets in Isola di Capo Rizzuto (3 km) and Le Castella (3 km). Also, during summer there are weekly markets with fresh local products. Each apartment's kitchen is fully equipped.

### 11. What payment methods are accepted?
**Answer:** We accept payments via bank transfer, PayPal, credit card, and cash (only for final balance). We require a 30% deposit to confirm the booking.

### 12. Is there a cleaning service during the stay?
**Answer:** Final cleaning is included. We also offer weekly cleaning service for a fee (on request). Bed and bath linen is included and changed weekly for long stays.

### 13. What is the best season to visit Capo Rizzuto?
**Answer:** Summer season (June-September) is ideal for sea and beach activities. May and October offer mild temperatures perfect for excursions and cultural visits. Winter is quiet and suitable for those seeking relaxation.

### 14. Are there restaurants nearby?
**Answer:** Yes, there are numerous restaurants in Le Castella (3 km) and Isola di Capo Rizzuto (3 km) serving fresh fish and Calabrian specialties. Many restaurants have terraces with sea views. The apartment kitchens are complete for preparing your own meals.

### 15. Can I organize events or parties?
**Answer:** Yes, upon request it's possible to organize private events, birthdays, or small celebrations. We ask you to let us know in advance to organize better. The pool area and garden are ideal for outdoor events.
